About

90Prime is a prime focus wide-field imager for the 90-inch telescope located at Kitt Peak. The camera’s focal plane array is populated with a mosaic of four thinned Lockheed 4096 × 4096 pixel CCDs. The f/2.98 system provides a plate scale of 0 .45/pixel and a total field-of-view of 1 .16° × 1.16°. The optical design includes a four element corrector and six position filter wheel. The first science run was conducted in November 2003.
